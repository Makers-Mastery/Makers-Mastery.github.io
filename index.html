About Me

Who I Am

I'm a robotics engineer and maker specializing in advanced prosthetics, humanoid robotics, and innovative mechanical design. I work at Make Salt Lake as a project assistant and maintenance technician, where I build cutting-edge projects in my downtime.

My work combines 3D printing, electronics, programming, and mechanical engineering to create unique solutions. From full-scale humanoid robots to prosthetic fingers with 3D-printed bone analogs, I push the boundaries of what's possible with maker technology.
